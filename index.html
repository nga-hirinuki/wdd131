About me

My name’s Ngatokoa, but my friends call me Nga. I was born in the Cook Islands and grew up in New Zealand. These days, I work for myself as a freelance graphic designer. My wife and I have four kids who are all grown up now. In my free time, I’m usually watching rugby or finding any excuse to break out the power tools—I’m always up for a good DIY project.

Auckland, New Zealand

New Zealand is crazy about rugby, and the All Blacks are legends—especially when they do the fierce haka before games. It was also the first country in the world to let women vote, way back in 1893. If you’re a movie fan, you’ll recognize its stunning landscapes from the “Lord of the Rings” films. With cool culture, epic views, and big achievements, New Zealand’s definitely a one-of-a-kind place.
